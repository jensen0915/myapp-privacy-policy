Quick Display Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to Quick Display, an Android app published by Jensen. We keep data handling minimal and do not operate a developer-owned server for this app.

2. Data Collection and Use

Quick Display does not collect, store, upload, sell, or share personal data. The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

3. Images and Local Files

Quick Display may allow the user to select images from the device or device storage so the app can display them. Selected images remain on the user's device and are not uploaded to the developer.

4. Internet Access

Quick Display does not require internet access for its core functionality and does not transmit user images or activity to a developer-owned server.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Because Quick Display does not collect personal data, there is no developer-side data retention. Users can remove local app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Quick Display is not specifically directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.

8. Contact

If you have questions, contact jensen0915@gmail.com.
