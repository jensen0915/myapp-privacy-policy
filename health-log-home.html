Health Log

A gentle personal health record app for Android.

Health Log helps users keep a simple local record of weight, body fat, blood pressure, heart rate, notes, and personal health goals. The app is designed as a low-pressure daily record tool, not as a medical diagnosis service.

健康紀錄是一款 Android 個人健康紀錄 App，可協助使用者以低壓力的方式記錄體重、體脂、血壓、心率、備註與個人目標。本 App 是日常紀錄工具，不提供醫療診斷。

Core features

  • Record weight, body fat percentage, blood pressure, heart rate, and notes.
  • Review recent records and gentle trend summaries.
  • Set personal goals and track progress as a habit-building journey.
  • Back up and restore records with Google Drive when the user chooses to connect Google.

Google Drive access

Google Drive access is optional. Health Log uses it only for backup and restore of the user's own Health Log records.

When connected, the app stores a backup file named health_records_backup.json in the user's Google Drive app data storage. This app data storage is managed by Google Drive and is not intended to appear as a normal user file in the main Drive file list.

使用者不連結 Google Drive 時，仍可使用本機健康紀錄功能。

Privacy and health information

Health data is stored on the user's device by default. The app does not upload records to a developer-operated server. Google Drive backup uses the user's own Google account storage after the user signs in.

Health Log is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or professional medical advice. Users should consult a qualified health professional for medical concerns.
