SnapScanCard

Business card scanning and contact memory for Android.

隱私權政策 (Privacy Policy) | 服務條款 (Terms of Service)

SnapScanCard helps users scan business cards, review OCR suggestions, organize contact details, attach meeting notes, and keep a local record of follow-up context.

SnapScanCard 是一款 Android 名片掃描與聯絡資料整理 App，可協助使用者掃描名片、檢查 OCR 辨識結果、整理聯絡資訊、記錄見面情境與後續追蹤狀態。

Core features

  • Scan or import a business card image and review OCR-recognized fields before saving.
  • Store names, companies, titles, phone numbers, email addresses, websites, addresses, tags, notes, and social links.
  • Attach person photos and business card images for later reference.
  • Use local export/import and optional manual Google Drive backup and restore.

Google Drive access

Google Drive access is optional and is used only when the user chooses to sign in and run a manual backup or restore.

The app requests Google Drive access to create and manage SnapScanCard backup ZIP files, including a dedicated backup folder named SnapScanCardBackup. The app does not use Google Drive for automatic sync, advertising, or unrelated file access.

Google Drive 備份是手動功能；使用者不登入 Google Drive 時，App 仍可在本機使用。

Privacy and data

Business card data is stored locally on the device by default. When the user exports or backs up data, the exported backup may include contact fields and saved images selected or created by the user.
