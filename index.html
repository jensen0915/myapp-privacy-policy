Jensen Apps Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to apps published by Jensen, including Quick Display and Health Log / 健康紀錄. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping our apps simple, transparent, and respectful of your data.

Different apps may handle different types of data depending on their features. The sections below explain how each app accesses, uses, stores, and shares data.

2. Apps Covered by This Policy

  • Quick Display
  • Health Log / 健康紀錄
  • Future apps published by Jensen, if they link to this Privacy Policy

3. Quick Display

3.1 Data Collection and Usage

Quick Display does not collect, store, process, upload, sell, or share any personal data, images, or user activity.

Quick Display does not require an internet connection and does not transmit any data externally.

No analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or third-party services are used in Quick Display.

3.2 Permissions

Quick Display may request permission to access images or device storage in order to display images selected by the user or available on the device. This access is used only for the app's display functionality.

Quick Display does not upload images, copy them to a remote server, or share them with the developer.

4. Health Log / 健康紀錄

4.1 Purpose of the App

Health Log / 健康紀錄 is a low-pressure health diary app that helps users record and review personal health-related data over time.

The app is not a medical system and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment advice, health risk assessment, or professional medical recommendations.

4.2 Data You May Enter

Health Log / 健康紀錄 may allow users to enter health-related records, including:

  • Weight
  • Body fat percentage
  • Blood pressure
  • Blood glucose
  • Heart rate
  • Notes entered by the user
  • Record date and time
  • Health goals, such as target weight or body fat percentage

4.3 Local Storage

By default, Health Log / 健康紀錄 stores your health records locally on your device.

The developer does not operate a separate server to collect, store, analyze, or share your health records.

4.4 Google Drive Backup

Health Log / 健康紀錄 may offer an optional Google Drive backup and restore feature. This feature is used only when the user chooses to connect a Google account and manually back up or restore data.

When Google Drive backup is enabled, the app stores backup data in the user's own Google Drive app data storage associated with the selected Google account. The backup may include health records and goal settings entered in the app.

Google Drive data is handled according to Google's own privacy policies and terms. The developer does not receive or store a copy of your Google Drive backup on a developer-owned server.

4.5 Google Account Access

Health Log / 健康紀錄 uses Google sign-in and Google Drive access only for the backup and restore feature.

The app does not use your Google account for advertising, profiling, analytics, or sharing with third parties.

4.6 Notifications

Health Log / 健康紀錄 may provide optional reminder notifications to help users remember to record their health data.

Reminder notifications are generated on the device and are not used for tracking or advertising.

5. Third-Party Services

Quick Display does not use third-party services that collect user data.

Health Log / 健康紀錄 may use Google services, including Google Sign-In and Google Drive, only for user-authorized backup and restore functionality.

Health Log / 健康紀錄 does not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics services.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data.

We do not share user data with advertisers, analytics providers, or developer-owned external servers.

If a user chooses to use Google Drive backup in Health Log / 健康紀錄, data is transferred to the user's own Google Drive account as part of the backup feature initiated by the user.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

7.1 Quick Display

Quick Display does not collect or retain user data.

7.2 Health Log / 健康紀錄

Health Log / 健康紀錄 stores records locally on the user's device unless the user chooses to use Google Drive backup.

Users can delete records inside the app. Users can also remove locally stored app data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage from Android system settings.

If Google Drive backup is used, users may delete the backup data from their Google account or Google Drive app data storage, subject to Google's available account and Drive management tools.

8. Data Security

We aim to keep data handling minimal. Quick Display does not transmit user data externally.

Health Log / 健康紀錄 stores health records locally by default. Optional Google Drive backup uses the user's own Google account and Google's storage infrastructure.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but we minimize data handling by avoiding developer-owned servers, advertising SDKs, and unnecessary third-party analytics.

9. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not specifically directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
